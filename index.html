Our Story

A cafe that refuses to sleep early

Tucked away on the first floor of Shree Building on Bhavani Shankar Road, Hungry Owl is Dadar's answer to late-night cravings. When everything else is shut, we're just getting started.

From cheesy loaded fries to hearty burgers and comfort pastas — every bite is made to satisfy those after-hours hunger pangs.
